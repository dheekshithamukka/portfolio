About Project

We are solving hunger and food wastage.

  • About Feed Hyderabad - Feed Hyderabad is a not-for-profit social organization which aims to solve the problem of hunger, malnutrition and food waste in the country. We channelize extra nutritious food from individuals, weddings, restaurants, corporate offices to the people who really need it and have no means or access food.
  • What is it about? - This project is based on donation of left-over food to poor organisations, orphanages etc., The main motto is stop hunger, zero food waste and balanced health diet.
  • Key Mission - STOP HUNGER, ZERO FOOD WASTE, HEALTHY BALANCED DIET
